ProxRemote for TV

Apple TV companion app for ProxRemote — read-only Proxmox VE cluster monitor.

tvOS 16+

What it does

Pair your iPhone once via QR code (end-to-end encrypted with X25519 + AES-GCM) and the TV shows live cluster status: nodes, virtual machines, containers, storage. Read-only by design — every destructive action lives in the iPhone app.
