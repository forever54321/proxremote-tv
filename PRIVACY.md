# ProxRemote for TV — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-12

## Data ProxRemote for TV stores on your device

- Paired server profiles (hostname, port, username, password) received from
  your iPhone via QR-code pairing
- App preferences (last-used server)

## Data ProxRemote for TV does NOT collect

- No analytics or telemetry
- No third-party SDKs or trackers
- No data is transmitted to ProxRemote servers (we have none)
- No data is shared, sold, or rented to any third party

## Network traffic

- The app only makes outbound TLS connections to the Proxmox server you
  paired from the iPhone
- The pairing exchange happens over your local network, end-to-end encrypted
  with X25519 + AES-GCM, so other devices on the same network cannot read the
  transmitted credentials

## Third-party services

- None

## Children

- ProxRemote for TV is not directed to children under 13.
